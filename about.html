Ablut Palago

Palago was created by Cameron Brown in 2008. It is a two player game with a total of 48 identical hexagonal tiles. Each tile contains two colors, blue and white. Each contains a straight and a circular tip of each color. Each player has to play two tiles each turn and the two tiles must be adjacent to each other. There are two ways of winning the game: (1) by forming a closed solid colored shape that contains at least one straight tip tile; (2) by being the last player to make a move.
